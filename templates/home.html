An Empty Mind is the Devil's Lair

When our minds are idle, they become susceptible to resurfacing insecurities and behaviors that swarm us within the drudgery of a mechanized world. Boredom, a state of mental inactivity, can exacerbate this vulnerability. In our daily lives, prolonged boredom can lead to unhealthy habits, anxiety, and even destructive actions as we seek stimulation. By staying engaged with meaningful activities and maintaining mental stimulation, we can prevent negative influences from taking hold and promote a healthier, more productive mindset.

Inspired by boredom, bored.ai is an initiative to tackle inactivity and the slow descent of motivating the inertial state and procastination. It aims to make this mindless state, something more "mindful".
