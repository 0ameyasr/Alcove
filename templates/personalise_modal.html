About Personalization

By using Personalization, you are allowing Alcove to analyse your chats, conversations and extract highlights, sentiments from past experiences. If you proceed, Alcove will begin to keep track of the previous few conversations and curate it's responses accordingly.

More than {{session["opted_users%"]}}% of our users have opted-in for Personalization.

How it works

When Personalization is on, Alcove analyses your past messages and conversations to improve the responses provided by Alcove; Here is how it works:
  1. Your history is being stored on our cloud, and undergoes constant analysis
  2. Your data and responses are analyzed to ensure the conversations remain relatable

Benefits of Personalization
  1. Chat companions remember your interests and things that you talk about.
  2. You can resume new conversations on the context of where you left before.
  3. Responses are more accurate and fitting to your style

If you're not using Personalization, chats will not retain inter-conversational text and your history. Each conversation would be treated uniquely.
